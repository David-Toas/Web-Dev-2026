Creamy Tuscan Chicken

Juicy chicken breasts in rich garlic-parmesan cream sauce with sun-dried tomatoes and fresh spinach.

Prep: 10 min Cook: 18 min Serves 4 Easy

Why You’ll Love It

One skillet, simple ingredients, restaurant-level flavor. Perfect for busy weeknights or when you want to impress without stress.

“Tastes like my favorite Italian restaurant — but better!”

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes (oil-packed, chopped)
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & black pepper

Nutrition (per serving)

Nutrient Amount % Daily Value
Calories 485 24%

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper and Italian seasoning. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken 5–6 min per side until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté garlic for 30 seconds (don’t let it burn).
  3. Add heavy cream, parmesan and sun-dried tomatoes. Simmer 2 minutes until slightly thickened.
  4. Stir in spinach until just wilted (≈ 1 minute).
  5. Return chicken to pan. Simmer 3–4 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F (74°C) internally.
  6. Serve immediately over pasta, rice or with bread.

Tips

  • Use thighs instead of breasts for more flavor
  • Add sliced mushrooms during garlic step
  • Make dairy-free with coconut cream + nutritional yeast
  • Leftovers keep well 2–3 days in the fridge
